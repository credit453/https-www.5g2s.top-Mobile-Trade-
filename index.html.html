    Description

    Your banking and financing are about to power up your lifestyle while making it easier to save for the future.



    Key app features:

    A UAE Bank Account:
    Open a UAE bank account from your phone with Al Maryah Community Bank, licensed by the Central Bank of the United Arab Emirates.



    Open in less than 5 minutes:
    The fastest access to banking and benefits – Your account will be functional in less than FIVE minutes due to seamless account opening experience.

    Gold for Free:
    Take advantage of no minimum balance and no annual fee for Gold Accounts.



    Remit or Receive Payments:
    Send money or receive payments to your current account.



    Get in Control:
    This is the one banking app that will give you control on spending limits and save.
